Get to Know Us

Rosé Noir is a Colorado Springs collective of wedding DJs, producers, and designers who believe sound is the emotional backbone of a celebration. We plan every transition so the night feels intentional rather than improvised.

Bespoke sound

From processional to final song, we tailor music to your taste, venue acoustics, and the arc of the evening.

Production that flatters

Lighting, speech clarity, and staging choices are designed to support the story—not distract from it.

Why Choose Rosé Noir

Luxury doesn’t mean loud; it means precise. Our approach balances energy with elegance so your guests feel guided, never rushed.

Cinematic pacing Prepared MCs Timeline stewardship Venue-aware audio Clean aesthetic

The Wedding Experience

We storyboard the night: curated playlists, seamless fades, tasteful lighting cues, and a dance floor that rises naturally after the key moments.

Guest-forward

Audio levels stay balanced so every toast is heard, while dance sets stay vibrant without fatigue.

Design-first

We pair lighting and visuals with your palette, allowing florals and attire to stay center stage.

Our Process

01. Discovery

We learn your vision, priorities, and non-negotiables so we can tailor the soundtrack and pacing.

02. Design

Lighting plots, audio plans, and curated playlists are built for your venue and flow.

03. Direction

On the day, we orchestrate cues with planners and vendors so each moment lands smoothly.

04. Encore

Post-event, we share highlights and refine preferences for future celebrations.
