Get to Know Us

Rosé Noir is a Colorado Springs–based collective of wedding DJs, producers, and designers who believe sound is the emotional backbone of a celebration.

Founded by Mark, Rosé Noir was created with a simple goal: to elevate wedding entertainment beyond playlists and presets. Every moment is planned with intention — from the processional to the final song — so the night feels cohesive, immersive, and effortless.

We approach weddings the same way we approach production: with care, preparation, and respect for the atmosphere you’re creating. That means listening first, designing thoughtfully, and executing seamlessly so you and your guests can stay present in the moment.

Rosé Noir exists for couples who value clarity over chaos, elegance over excess, and an experience that feels refined rather than rushed.

Why Choose Rosé Noir

Sound with intention

Music isn’t background noise — it’s pacing, energy, and emotion. We design every transition to feel deliberate, guiding the room naturally from one moment to the next.

Production that supports the story

Lighting, microphones, and staging are chosen to enhance the experience, not overpower it. Every technical decision serves the atmosphere.

Visuals that elevate, never distract

When video and visual elements are used, they’re purposeful and restrained — adding depth without pulling focus from what matters most.

A calm, professional presence

Behind the scenes, we operate with precision so the celebration itself feels effortless. No scrambling. No guesswork. Just confidence.

The Wedding Experience

Every Rosé Noir wedding begins long before the first song plays.

We take time to understand your vision, your venue, and the emotional arc of the day — from ceremony to last dance. Planning is collaborative, thoughtful, and unrushed.

On the day itself, our role is simple: execute flawlessly and stay invisible when needed. Transitions feel natural. Sound remains clear. Lighting complements the room.

The result is a wedding that feels composed, immersive, and entirely yours — one where the technology disappears and the experience remains.

Our Process

01. Discovery

We learn your vision, priorities, and non-negotiables so we can tailor the soundtrack and pacing.

02. Design

Lighting plots, audio plans, and curated playlists are built for your venue and flow.

03. Direction

On the day, we orchestrate cues with planners and vendors so each moment lands smoothly.

04. Encore

Post-event, we share highlights and refine preferences for future celebrations.
